## About Me

I am a Research Fellow at AI Singapore, under the National University of Singapore. I am also working in the Group of Learning and Optimization Working in AI, under Associate Professor Bryan Low Kian Hsiang. 

Previously, I completed my PhD degree from the School of Computing, at the National University of Singapore. I was supervised by Associate Professor Stéphane Bressan. My dissertation topic was on Physics Informed Machine Learning and Dynamical Systems. 

My current research interests sit at the intersection of AI and the social sciences. I am interested in understanding how technology affects our daily lives, and in exploring the technical interventions we can design to address its broader social impacts. This interest is shaped by my undergraduate background in mechanical engineering and economics, as well as my PhD research in AI, which together inform how I think about both technological systems and their societal consequences.
